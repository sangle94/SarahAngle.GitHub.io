Sarah Angle

I'm currently a mechanical engineering student, focusing on control systems and robotics, at Cornell University in Ithaca, NY. At school I help teach freshman courses, do undergraduate research, and play competitive ultimate frisbee (go Roses!)

Since starting college I've found myself intrigued by mechatronics. From personal projects like a record player to work projects like an exoskeleton glove, I've explored motors, arduino, sensors, and everything in between. Most of my projects are based on non-academic interests as well - like the headphone tube amp I'm building to help listen to music.

Outside of listening to music, I play a few instruments, but guitar is my current favorite. I also love to read authors like Vonnegut and Hemingway. But most of all, I love to be outside, and most weekends I find time to backpack, hike, bike, backcountry ski, or nap in a hammock.
