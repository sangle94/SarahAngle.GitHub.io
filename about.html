Sarah Angle

I'm an engineer based out of San Francisco. While my degree and experience focuses on the mechanical side of things, I love working with electronics and software as well. Robotics and mechatronics have been a major interest of mine since studying in college - and luckily I got to work on those projects when I interned at Autodesk in 2015. Now, I'm working on product design in IOT/consumer electronics with Fitbit's Advance Product Development group.

Designing and making things is not just my profession, it's also a hobby. On rainy weekends, I'm most often found drinking coffee and working on a personal project - right now I'm making a bike computer from scratch, based around a Nordic nRF52 chip. Otherwise I'll be playing guitar or reading around the house - I'm a huge fan of Vonnegut. I also like to read books about popular science and conservation.

But on sunny days, I love to be outside. You can probably find me somewhere around California most weekends, playing ultimate frisbee, biking, backpacking, backcountry skiing, or napping in a hammock.
